Refund and Cancellation Policy

Transparent refund timelines and cancellation rules for future service payments.

Zerotonin Innovations Private Limited believes in providing transparent and fair business practices.

Refund Policy

Refunds will be considered only in the following cases:

  • Duplicate payment
  • Payment failure (amount debited but service not confirmed)
  • Non-delivery of agreed services

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to the original mode of payment.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation requests must be raised within 24 hours of payment by contacting our support team.
